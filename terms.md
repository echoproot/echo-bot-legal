## 2. What Echo does

Echo is a hobby entertainment bot for Discord servers. Its features include:

- **Roleplay and fun commands** — `/boop`, `/hug`, `/kiss`, `/scan`, `/beep`, `/bluescreen`, `/feed`, `/quote`, `/proto`, which reply with text, images or GIFs.
- **Utility commands** — `/avatar` (displays a Discord user's public profile picture) and `/status` (server information).
- **Music playback** — `/play`, `/skip`, `/stop`, `/volume`, which stream audio into a voice channel.
- **Conversational AI** — an optional dedicated channel, configured with `/setup_ai`, where Echo replies in character.
- **Configuration commands** — `/setup_echo`, `/setup_music`, `/setup_ai`, which link Echo's features to a channel of your choice.

Features may be added, changed or removed at any time without notice.
